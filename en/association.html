Bylaws of The Violas Association

A Governor is the executive branch of a Violas State. A Governor manages its validator node on the Violas Blockchain.

A Violas Validator

A Violas Validator Node is a member of the Violas Senate, while Representatives are the legislative branch of a Violas State and members of the Violas House of Representatives.

A Violas Representative

A Violas Representative is elected by the stablecoin issuers within a given Violas State. The number of Representatives each Violas State has is proportional to the size of its ecosystem.
