Members of the Association

100 Members of the “Violas States”

The association will be a network of 100 members of “Violas States”, each of which is the Governor of a “Violas State”to serve as a validator node of Violas Blockchain, and contributes 100 Violas Investment Tokens (a token that grants rights to a share of the future revenue generated from the Violas Blockchain and its Ecosystem). 1 Violas Investment Token = 1 bitcoin (BTC). Members of the association drive the development of the Violas Blockchain, the management of the Violas Reserve, and the approach to build Violas Ecosystem.

500 Representatives of Stablecoin Issuers

Each “Violas State” Representative is elected within a community of Issuers of Stablecoin Offering. Representatives join with the Governor to drive the development of the Violas Blockchain, the management of the Violas Reserve, and the approach to build each “Violas State” Ecosystem as well as sharing the revenue generated from the Violas Blockchain and its Ecosystem.

Custodial Services for Compliant Assets

Institutions as the custodian for Issuers of Stablecoin Offering assets requires to have compliance with local asset holding requirements in funds management and custodial services and established reputation certified by the association. They may charge asset management fee for the service.

Issuers of stablecoin offering

Issuer of Stablecoin Offering could be an organization or an individual person. The required reserve of assets to issue stablecoin must be either local fiat currency or low-risk short-term bonds certified by the association.

Issuers of stablecoin offering must guarantee the stable value of reserve assets which may only experience minor fluctuations, and are obligated to disclose and replenish assets when the reserves are insufficient.

The Stablecoin Offering must comply with local laws and regulations.

Issuers of stablecoin offering can earn interest of reserve asset and revenue generated from the circulation of stablecoin.

Application Developer

Application Developers have to negotiate an agreement with issuer of stablecoin offering use stablecoin and comply with local laws and regulations.
